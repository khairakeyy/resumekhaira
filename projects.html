Projects

Surat Kita

Surat Kita was developed during a two-week Hackathon organized by Ko+Lab. In this competition, we designed an automated solution for sending official letters using Google Apps Script, allowing users to efficiently and systematically send formal documents through Google Docs and Gmail. The project aims to simplify administrative processes, particularly in academic and organizational environments.

Mockup Product

Assisted the PKM team in creating product design materials, including packaging mockups and logos, for an innovation based on betel leaf (daun sirih). The designs were created using Canva, focusing on a natural, eco-friendly, and modern aesthetic to support the branding of bioplastic products derived from betel leaves. This project was related to PKM activities at Telkom University in February 2025.

Poster Design

Created a poster with the theme "Quality Education" using Canva for a poster design competition. The poster aimed to educate and inspire awareness about the importance of inclusive and quality education. Although it did not win the competition, this project provided valuable experience in enhancing graphic design skills and delivering effective visual messages.
